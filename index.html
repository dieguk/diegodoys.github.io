Some of my Recent Projects

NurseEye

NurseEye is an application for the evaluation, monitoring, and tracking of chronic wounds such as diabetic, pressure, and cardiovascular ulcers. It allows for graphing the results and facilitates therapeutic decision-making providing automatic PDF reports with photographic documentation.

3D planning and guides for surgeries

More than 50 shoulder, elbow, and sternum surgeries, with excellent results, being pioneers in Hispanic America in trauma surgeries. The guides are designed and 3D manufactured with antimicrobial and biocompatible materials, combining anatomical knowledge with 3D design.

ExoHand

ExoHand is a simplified dynamic orthosis that facilitates hand and wrist extension movements, reducing the load on this musculature and preventing the development of tendinopathies such as lateral epicondylitis. It is designed for intensive computer use in work environments, but once it was released to the market, it has been used for therapy, chronic pain, and neurorehabilitation.
